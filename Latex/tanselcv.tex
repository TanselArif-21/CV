\section{Research Experience}
\cventry{2011 -- 2015}{Imperial College London}{}{UK}{}{\normalsize 
\begin{itemize}
\item[-] The focus during my PhD research has been on the development of theory and code for the phase-field modelling and simulation of microstructures found in steel~\hyperref[refbain]{[1,2]} as well as the formation of van der Waals fluids using the smoothed particle hydrodynamics method. 
\item[-] Given my interest in the prediction of general evolutionary phenomena, I have collaborated on cellular automata treatment for solidification~\hyperref[refmeas]{[3]}.
